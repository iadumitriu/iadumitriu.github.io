Places I've Worked

As the Community Benefit Specialist at Presence Health, I quantify the benefits that our hospitals deliver to our communities, from uncompensated care to community health programs. By rigorously evaluating, predicting, and visualizing the services we provide, I help identify ways to proactively improve our patients' health while costing less money for themselves, Presence, and government payors.

As a Master in Public Policy student at The Harris School of Public Policy Studies at the University of Chicago, I studied health policy and program evaluation, with a special focus on data analysis in public policy. Through the Graduate Program in Health Administration and Policy I worked on an interdisciplinary case study for a large health provider in Chicago. As a Research Assistant for Brett Goldstein, the former CIO of the City of Chicago, I was the product manager for Plenario, refined an algorithm for smart spatial clustering, and worked with members of Chicago's tech community.
